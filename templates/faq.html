Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the login and fill out the required information.

3. How do I join a channel?

To join a channel, click on the "Join Channel" button and enter the invitation link or code provided by the channel owner.

4. Is my data secure?

We take security seriously. Your data is encrypted and stored securely.

5. Can I customize my profile?

Yes, you can customize your profile by adding a profile picture, changing your username, and updating your status.

6. How do I invite friends to join?

You can invite friends by sending them an invitation link or by sharing the channels's name and invite code.
