Getting Started

  • Channels

    Channels are where you'll find discussions organized by topic. Click on a channel to view its messages and participate in the conversation.

  • Messaging

    To send a message, simply type your text in the message input box at the bottom of the screen and press Enter. You can also send direct messages to other users by clicking on their name and selecting "Message".

  • Settings

    Adjust your account settings to personalize your experience. You can change your username, profile picture, and more from the settings menu.
